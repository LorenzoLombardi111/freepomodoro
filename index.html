Free Pomodoro Timer — Focus Better with Timed Work Sessions

freepomodoro.app is a free online Pomodoro timer that helps you stay focused using timed work sessions and regular breaks. Based on the Pomodoro Technique, freepomodoro.app guides you through 25-minute focus sessions followed by 5-minute short breaks, with a longer 15-minute break after every four sessions. All timer durations are fully customizable, and your session history is tracked privately in your browser. freepomodoro.app requires no sign-up, shows no ads, and works entirely offline as a progressive web app. Whether you are studying, coding, writing, or working through tasks, freepomodoro.app keeps you on pace without distractions.

How does the Pomodoro Technique work with freepomodoro.app?

Select Focus mode and press the play button to start a 25-minute work session. When the timer ends, a sound plays and freepomodoro.app automatically transitions to a short break. After four focus sessions, you get a longer break. You can customize every duration in the settings panel. Your completed sessions and total focus time are tracked throughout the day.

Is freepomodoro.app free to use?

freepomodoro.app is completely free with no sign-up required. All timer logic runs locally in your browser. There are no ads, no tracking cookies, and no usage limits.
